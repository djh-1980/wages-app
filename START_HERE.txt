================================================================================
COMMON TASKS
================================================================================

View quick stats:
  python3 quick_stats.py

Search for specific data:
  python3 query_payslips.py

Export to Excel:
  python3 export_to_csv.py
  (Then open the .csv files in Excel)

Generate full report:
  python3 generate_report.py
  (Creates payslip_report.txt)

Add new payslips:
  1. Add PDF files to PaySlips/2024/ or PaySlips/2025/
  2. Run: python3 extract_payslips.py
