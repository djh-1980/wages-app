Getting Started with HMRC MTD
What is Making Tax Digital?

Making Tax Digital (MTD) is HMRC's initiative to digitize the tax system. Self-employed individuals must submit quarterly updates of income and expenses digitally.

Setup Steps:
  1. Register with HMRC: Sign up for MTD at gov.uk
  2. Get Developer Credentials: Create an application at HMRC Developer Hub
  3. Configure Environment Variables: Add your Client ID and Client Secret to the .env file
  4. Connect: Click "Connect to HMRC" to authorize the application
  5. Enter Details: Provide your NINO and Business ID
  6. Submit Quarterly: Use the Expenses page to submit quarterly updates
Environment Variables Required:
HMRC_CLIENT_ID=your-client-id
HMRC_CLIENT_SECRET=your-client-secret
HMRC_REDIRECT_URI=http://localhost:5000/api/hmrc/auth/callback
HMRC_ENVIRONMENT=sandbox  # or 'production'
Quarterly Deadlines:
  • Q1: April 6 - July 5 (Due: August 5)
  • Q2: July 6 - October 5 (Due: November 5)
  • Q3: October 6 - January 5 (Due: February 5)
  • Q4: January 6 - April 5 (Due: May 5)
Important Notes
  • Always test in sandbox environment first
  • Keep your credentials secure and never commit them to version control
  • Quarterly submissions are mandatory - late submissions may incur penalties
  • Review all data before submitting to HMRC
