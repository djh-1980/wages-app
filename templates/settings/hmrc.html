Help & Documentation
What is Making Tax Digital?

Making Tax Digital (MTD) is HMRC's initiative to digitize the tax system. Self-employed individuals must submit quarterly updates of income and expenses digitally.

MTD Process Overview
  1. Connect to HMRC: Authorize this application to access your MTD account
  2. Submit Quarterly Updates: Submit income and expenses for each quarter (Q1-Q4)
  3. Trigger Tax Calculation: After all 4 quarters, request HMRC to calculate your tax
  4. Submit Final Declaration: Confirm all information is correct and complete
  5. Pay Tax: Pay any tax owed by the deadline (31 January)
